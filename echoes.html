The Legend of Zelda: Echoes of Wisdom

The intent of this project was to improve the infamous Echoes Menu, whereby the player has to spend
countless seconds scrolling left and right to find the specific echo they are looking for in one long, horizontal list.
This UI fix adds in five principal categories, while allowing the player to still toggle between most and last used.
