Easy Rental

Choose your location, date, time, duration, and number of boats. On the day of your rental, find a space in the park away from others and wait for a text or call letting you know your boat is ready. Follow the directional signs to get to your paddle and PFDs.

Three Locations

Three great locations to serve you. Wingra Boats on Knickerbocker Street, Brittingham Boats at Brittingham Place, and Marshall Boats on Allen Boulevard.

Boats and More

Join us on the lake! We have something for all boat lovers, from canoes to single and tandem kayaks. Stand up paddle boards, paddle boats, duck paddle boats, as well as pontoon boat rentals!
