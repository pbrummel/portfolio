Beautiful gardens are the heart of any community

Willow Gardens provides an entry point for individuals and households looking to get involved in the whole project. Community gardeners use the land to grow their own food, which has a positive impact on their household security. We provide outdoor activity as well as a fun and friendly environment for people of all ages, incomes, and cultures to work the land together and share their gardening knowledge and ideas. Gardeners can also assist in site maintenance and management through volunteering.

There are plenty of very nice garden spots available. Click here to buy a plot!
